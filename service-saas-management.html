The problem we solve

Sprawl. Waste. Renewal opacity. Embedded AI cost.

Sprawl — hundreds of distinct SaaS apps, most procured outside formal channels, many redundant, few governed. Sprawl is the root cause; the other three problems flow from it.

Waste — dormant seats, over-tiered editions, unused modules, departing employees still consuming licences, AppExchange apps no one remembers buying. Recurring; accumulates fastest in tools that auto-provision.

Renewal opacity — SaaS contracts auto-renew. Few enterprises systematically prepare for renewal until the vendor's account team initiates the conversation — and by then the negotiation window has narrowed.

Embedded AI cost — every major SaaS vendor (ServiceNow Now Assist, Salesforce Agentforce, Microsoft Copilot, Adobe Firefly, Atlassian Intelligence) now layers AI add-ons into the renewal. Each is its own pricing motion, each needs its own value case.

What's included

Six work-streams. One outcome.

Every licence optimisation engagement covers these areas — adapted in scale and depth to your environment, contracts, and timeline.

— 01

SaaS estate discovery

Discovery of every active SaaS application — including shadow IT. We pull from SSO logs, finance, expense reports, and SaaS management platforms (Zylo, Productiv, Torii, Flexera One SaaS) where in place.

— 02

Utilisation analysis

Per-application analysis of seat utilisation, edition fitness, module usage, and dormancy. Output: vendor-by-vendor right-sizing recommendations with quantified savings.

— 03

Renewal calendar & negotiation playbook

Twelve-month renewal calendar with kick-off triggers set well ahead of vendor cycles. Each major vendor gets a dedicated negotiation playbook: pricing benchmarks, escalation paths, alternative-vendor positioning.

— 04

Embedded-AI strategy

Vendor-by-vendor view of how each SaaS platform is monetising AI, what the value case actually looks like for your environment, and how to negotiate the AI line items at renewal — without paying for capabilities that don't yet move the needle.

— 05

Vendor consolidation analysis

Mapping of functional overlap across the SaaS estate. Where multiple vendors solve the same problem, we model the consolidation trade-offs and recommend which to scale, which to retire, and which to renegotiate.

— 06

Ongoing managed service

Recurring optimisation cadence — monthly utilisation reviews, quarterly business reviews with vendors, annual portfolio rationalisation. Designed to keep savings recurring rather than one-off.
