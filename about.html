About Proteam

Nobody knows SAM like we do.

We're a specialist Software Asset Management firm — built by SAM veterans, focused on a single discipline, and accountable for one thing: outcomes you can take to the CFO.

Our story

SAM is the discipline. Outcomes are the deliverable.

We don't sell licences. We don't take vendor commissions. We work for you — period.

Most companies treat software licensing as an admin task — something procurement can handle alongside negotiating coffee contracts. It isn't. A single Oracle audit can dwarf a year's procurement savings. A misread IBM contract clause can cost millions. The skills required to optimise software estates and defend audits aren't peripheral to procurement or IT — they are a discipline of their own.

That discipline is what we practice. Proteam Advisory was founded by senior consultants who had spent careers at the Big Four and the world's best-known SAM consultancies — and who saw, repeatedly, that enterprises were being underserved. Generalists were running specialist work. Junior staff were being shipped onto complex audits. Vendor influence was creeping into "independent" advisory.

We built Proteam to fix that. Specialists only. Every consultant on our team has years of SAM-specific experience, deep familiarity with the major vendors' contract languages, and a track record of measurable savings. When a CFO asks "why are we paying for this engagement?", our consultants can show the answer in numbers.

Today we serve over 500 enterprise clients across India, the UK, the US, the Middle East, and continental Europe. We've led more than 4,000 software compliance reviews. Our clients have collectively saved over $1 billion through our work — and we maintain a 100% client satisfaction record across every engagement.

We work inside whatever SAM tooling you've already invested in. We're vendor-agnostic but vendor-fluent. We anchor engagements to outcomes, not hours. And we offer a free, no-obligation SAM consultation to anyone serious about understanding their current risk position — because we'd rather show our work than oversell it.

How we work

Five principles, uncompromised.

— five principles
— 01

Independent, always

We take no commissions, no kickbacks, and no referral fees from software vendors. The advice we give you is the advice we'd give ourselves. Your interests are the only interests on the table.

— 02

Specialists, not generalists

SAM is all we do. Every team member has held senior positions at the Big Four or other specialist consultancies. No junior bench. No upskilling on your engagement.

— 03

Outcomes over hours

We anchor commercial models to measurable savings and reduced audit exposure — not to time-and-materials. If we don't move the number, we shouldn't get paid for it.

— 04

Tool-flexible

We work inside whatever SAM tooling you've already invested in. No rip-and-replace agendas, no preferred-vendor pressure. We make your existing stack work harder.

— 05

Honest assessment first

Our free SAM consultation isn't a sales call. If we don't think we can save you meaningful money or reduce real risk, we'll tell you so — and recommend what to do instead.

— 06

Confidentiality, contractually

What we see inside your environment stays inside your environment. We treat your contracts, deployment data, and audit history with the seriousness our clients expect.
