The problem we solve

Without a baseline, everything else is guessing.

Optimisation, audit defence, contract negotiation, SAM tooling investment — every one of these activities depends on having an accurate, current, defensible picture of what software you actually own and what you actually use. That picture is your baseline.

Most enterprises don't have one. They have spreadsheets that are out of date the moment they're saved. SAM tools producing reports nobody trusts. Departmental data sources that don't agree with each other. The result: every licensing decision is made on shaky ground.

We build that ground. Forensic baselining first — across whichever vendors matter to you — followed by ongoing managed services that keep the baseline current, surface emerging risks, and continuously identify new optimisation opportunities. Continuously audit-ready, not periodically panicked.

What's included

Six work-streams. One outcome.

Every licence optimisation engagement covers these areas — adapted in scale and depth to your environment, contracts, and timeline.

— 01

Forensic baselining

Vendor-by-vendor reconstruction of your true licence position. We unpack contracts, validate entitlements, reconcile deployments, and produce a defensible baseline that holds up to vendor scrutiny.

— 02

Deployment discovery

We work inside your existing SAM tooling — Flexera, ServiceNow, Snow, USU, Aspera, Eracent — and supplement with custom discovery for vendors and environments your tool doesn't fully cover.

— 03

Contract reconciliation

Every active agreement is reconciled against deployment data. Discrepancies, ambiguous clauses, and historical amendments are documented — so you know exactly where the contractual ground is firm and where it isn't.

— 04

Monthly compliance reporting

Recurring reports that show, vendor by vendor, your current licence position, deviations from baseline, emerging risks, and the cost of remediation — written in language a CFO can read.

— 05

Optimisation watch-list

The managed service doesn't stop at compliance. We continuously surface optimisation opportunities — unused licences, over-tiered editions, renewal positioning — that translate into recurring cost reductions.

— 06

Helpdesk SLA

On-demand access to senior SAM specialists for any licensing question your IT, procurement, or finance teams encounter — renewals, M&A licence implications, deployment changes, vendor disputes — typically within 24 hours.

How we work

Baseline first. Then never let it slip.

Initial baselining typically runs 6–12 weeks. Managed services begin immediately afterwards, on a monthly operating cadence.

01

Scope & discover

We agree which vendors are in scope, which environments matter, and what defensibility standard the baseline needs to meet. Critical contracts are pulled. Existing SAM tool data is connected.

02

Baseline & validate

Forensic reconstruction of your licence position, vendor by vendor. Entitlements validated against contracts. Deployments reconciled against entitlement. Discrepancies documented and quantified.

03

Stabilise & onboard

Outstanding compliance gaps remediated. Governance processes installed. Reporting cadence agreed. Helpdesk SLAs activated. Your environment is now under continuous SAM management.

04

Run & evolve

Monthly reporting, quarterly business reviews, on-demand specialist support. The baseline never goes stale, the optimisation watch-list never stops growing, and the next vendor audit finds nothing to complain about.
